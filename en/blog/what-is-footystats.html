What is FootyStats and what is it for?

FootyStats is a football statistics website built specifically for bettors. With over a million monthly visitors, it's one of the most complete tools on the market: it covers more than 1500 leagues and competitions worldwide, from the English Premier League to regional tournaments in Asia and the Americas.

Unlike generic sites such as Soccerway or WhoScored, FootyStats has a declared betting focus: every stat is presented with the bettor in mind, not just fans following scores.

What data does it offer?

The platform goes far beyond results. Inside every match and team you'll find:

  • Over/Under goals: goals-per-match averages and trends for over/under 1.5, 2.5 and 3.5
  • xG (Expected Goals): how many goals each team should have scored given chance quality
  • Corner stats: averages, trends, home vs away performance
  • Cards: yellows and reds per match, referee, home/away condition
  • H2H (Head to Head): full history between teams — over 600,000 comparisons available
  • Automated predictions: a proprietary system that flags matches meeting specific statistical criteria
  • User performance: you can follow the picks of the platform's most profitable bettors
  • Historical data: up to 5 years of history per team to spot real trends

The predictions: how do they work?

One of the most useful features of FootyStats is its automated prediction system. Rather than just showing historical stats, the platform automatically flags today's matches that meet validated statistical criteria — for example, "both teams hit over 1.5 in their last 5 home/away matches".

The result is a filtered list of matches with strong statistical backing for specific markets: over/under, both teams to score (BTTS), corners, cards. They're not result predictions — they're statistical filters, which is exactly what a value bettor needs.

The betting systems

  • Under X In-Play system: +288% ROI over 600 tested bets
  • Late Corners system: +470% ROI over 37 bets

These are backtested historical results, not future guarantees. But the transparency of showing every system's full history — losses included — is something valuable that not every service offers.

Is it free?

FootyStats has a solid free version with access to core stats, predictions and historical data for the main leagues. The Premium plan unlocks the full 1500+ leagues, the betting systems, advanced filters, per-league corner and card data, and CSV export.

Which type of bettor is it for?

FootyStats works for any bettor who builds decisions on statistics. It's especially useful if you:

  • Bet on alternative markets: corners, cards, over/under instead of just the result
  • Hunt for second or third division leagues where bookmakers have less information
  • Want long-term historical data before validating a strategy
  • Like following the platform's most profitable bettors to study their criteria
