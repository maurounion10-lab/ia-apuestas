What is Adam Choi?

Adam Choi (adamchoi.co.uk) is one of the most popular football statistics tools among professional bettors. Unlike generic platforms, Adam Choi was designed from scratch so anyone can spot statistical patterns visually — no advanced data-analysis skills required.

What makes Adam Choi unique is its red-and-green colour system. You don't need to interpret complex numbers or percentages: the colour tells you instantly whether a team, player or market is in form or not. That's why it's the go-to tool for bettors who value clarity and speed.

The red/green system: the most intuitive interface

The distinctive feature of Adam Choi is its colour coding:

  • Green: a positive trend, high probability, or a pattern that favours the bet
  • Red: a negative trend, low probability, or a pattern working against it
  • Yellow/Neutral: no clear trend, insufficient data, or mixed signals

This visual system is so effective that many bettors only need to glance at the colour to make decisions. Of course Adam Choi also shows the numbers behind it, but the interface prioritises visualisation over raw figures.

Which markets does Adam Choi cover?

The platform covers far more than 1X2 results:

  • BTTS (Both Teams To Score): the probability both sides score, with a 5-10 match history
  • Over/Under: goal trends, over or under 1.5, 2.5 and 3.5
  • Corners: per-team averages, home vs away trends, in-match corner distribution
  • Cards: yellows and reds by team, referee, and home/away condition
  • Streaks: how many matches a team has gone unbeaten, scoring, conceding, etc.
  • Player Markets: individual player stats — goals, assists, shots on target, cards

The 39 leagues covered

Adam Choi covers 39 major leagues, including:

  • The big five: Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1
  • Second divisions of the main countries
  • Smaller European leagues: Portugal, Netherlands, Belgium, Scotland, etc.
  • Competitions: Champions League, Europa League, domestic cups
  • International leagues: MLS, Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, etc.

Adam Choi stats update in real time, from the Premier League down to lower divisions.

Free vs Premium: what does paying unlock?

Adam Choi has a completely free version with access to all leagues, the red/green system, core stats, BTTS, corners and cards. For bettors starting out, the free tier is enough.

The Premium plan ($4.99-9.99 USD/month depending on country) unlocks:

  • Full Player Markets with advanced filters
  • Odds filtering: see which bookmakers offer the best value for your picks
  • Individual player highlights with their stats
  • Deeper historical data
  • Minute-by-minute match analysis

💡 Most professional bettors we know use the FREE version of Adam Choi. Premium adds features, but they're not essential if your strategy is based on team statistics.

Which type of bettor is Adam Choi for?

Adam Choi is the perfect tool if you:

  • Want fast visual analysis without complicated numbers
  • Bet on alternative markets: BTTS, corners, cards, over/under
  • Need to filter leagues and trends in 30 seconds
  • Want to validate historical patterns before placing a bet
  • Follow specific players or smaller leagues where value hides

It's not ideal if you need ready-to-use predictions (that's Forebet's job) or very deep xG analysis (that's FootyStats).
