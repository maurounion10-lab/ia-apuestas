What gambeta.ai is

gambeta.ai is a free football betting predictions platform powered by artificial intelligence, founded in Argentina. It publishes daily picks for 38+ leagues — Premier League, La Liga, Champions League, Brasileirão, Copa Libertadores, MLS and more — each with a market, odds, a 1-6 confidence level and a suggested stake.

How the AI generates picks

  1. Data processing. For every match, the system processes 40+ variables: market odds and their movement, confirmed line-ups and injuries, head-to-head records, home/away form, expected goals (xG) and even each squad's market value (via Transfermarkt data).
  2. Probability estimation. Machine-learning models estimate the real probability of each outcome, independently of the bookmaker.
  3. Value detection. The AI compares its estimate with the probability implied by the odds. A pick is only published when its estimate beats the market's — that gap is the mathematical value.

A track record nobody can edit

Every pick is published before kick-off and its result (win, loss or void) is resolved automatically by a server-side system that checks final scores from independent sports data sources every hour. Results are public in the Results section: as of July 2026, ~600 resolved picks with a ~62% win rate and +673 units of cumulative profit at suggested stakes. Postponed or cancelled matches are voided with the stake returned. Nothing is deleted.

🤖 The whole pipeline is automated: pick generation, publication, score resolution and daily results reporting run on scheduled cloud jobs — no human touches the record.

What it costs

Nothing. gambeta.ai is 100% free, with no VIP tiers and no registration required to see the picks. The project is sustained by its sponsor (DBbet) and affiliate links to regulated tools. A free account optionally unlocks results tracking, a bankroll manager and a Telegram alert bot.

What it doesn't do

It doesn't guarantee profits — no honest prediction system can. Betting always involves risk; the AI tilts the odds in your favour over the long run but doesn't remove variance. 18+, always gamble responsibly and only with money you can afford to lose.
